📝 Conclusion

This investigation confirmed a successful phishing attack resulting in endpoint compromise. The attacker employed social engineering via a fraudulent Windows 11 Pro upgrade offer. The victim clicked the embedded link, visited the malicious domain windows-update.site (confirmed malicious via VirusTotal), and their system was compromised by Lumma Stealer through DLL side-loading.

Key Takeaways: Email senders and embedded links must be verified before interaction. Free or urgent offers are common phishing lures. Threat intelligence platforms such as VirusTotal are essential for validating suspicious IPs, domains, and links. Endpoint compromise was confirmed through multiple independent sources (email analysis, website inspection, terminal history extraction, VirusTotal correlation, and process analysis). The affected system was contained and escalated to Tier 2 for further remediation.
